What Drives Us

Three Things We'll Never Compromise On.

Freshness Above All

Every batch is made the morning of delivery. No freezing. No cold-chain shortcuts. The gap between production and your kitchen is measured in hours, not days.

Real Ingredients Only

Non-GMO soybeans, water, and our own spice blends. That's the complete list. No stabilisers, no artificial flavours, no MSG, no preservatives. What you read is what you eat.

Flavour as a Standard

Healthy food doesn't get to be boring. We develop our flavours — from Kandhari chilli heat to Peri Peri tang — until they stand up to anything in a Kerala kitchen.

How We Got Here

The Amel Journey

Chapter 1

The Frustration

We were health-conscious people living in Malappuram — and the tofu options available to us were either bland, poorly made, or nowhere near the quality of the bold food we grew up with. We couldn't find tofu that felt like it belonged in a Kerala kitchen.

Chapter 2

The Experiment

We started making our own. Small batches. Testing with friends. Iterating on textures and spice profiles until we landed on something that genuinely excited us. The first Kandhari Nuggets batch convinced us we were onto something real.

Chapter 3

Amel Is Born

We formalised the brand, set up our small production facility in Karuvarakundu, and launched with a core range. The name is a promise — not a compromise. Every product that carries the Amel name has to earn it.

Today

Kerala's Premium Tofu Brand

We now offer 3 core products — Firm Tofu Block, Tofu Nuggets (in 3 flavours), and Tofu Broast — all made fresh every day in Malappuram and delivered across Kerala. We're just getting started.

Farm to Pack

We Know Where Every Bean Comes From.

Our soybeans are sourced from certified farms that share our standards — non-GMO, no synthetic pesticides, full traceability. We visit our suppliers personally and keep our supply chain tight on purpose.

From soybean to sealed pack, every Amel product is made in our Malappuram facility. No outsourcing, no co-manufacturing, no guessing about what's going into your food.
